Privacy

How Sonto handles your data.

Data Collection

Sonto collects no personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no telemetry.

Local Storage

All saved snippets and their vector embeddings are stored in your browser's IndexedDB. Nothing is sent to any server. Embeddings are generated locally using a WASM-compiled model.

API Keys

Your API keys are stored locally using chrome.storage.local. They are only used to authenticate your chat requests directly to the AI provider you selected.

AI Chat

When you ask a question, relevant snippet text is sent to your chosen AI provider. Review their privacy policies:

  • OpenAI
  • Google AI
  • xAI (Grok)
