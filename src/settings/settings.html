Privacy

All clipboard entries are stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB. Nothing is sent to any server. No accounts, no telemetry, no analytics.

Third-Party Content

The Zen feed displays content from public APIs (museums, news aggregators, quote services, and others). Sonto does not own or curate this content.
